Return Policies

Returns of Namstey retail product must be taken care of at the store at which it was purchased. 
No Return, No Exchanges, No Credit For Sale Items, Retired Fragrances, Flash Sale Items Or Clearance Items.

  1. All current, full priced merchandise purchased from NamsteyCandle.com can be returned for a replacement or a refund within 30 days from purchase date. 
  2. Credits for returns will be refunded to the original credit card used to place the order. Refunds can take 7 - 10 business days, once the credit has been requested. 
  3. Please note, the returned products must be in the original packaging, unused, and in sellable condition. If you no longer have your packing slip, please put all of the information on a separate sheet of paper, including your order number, mailing address and phone number, along with the item description, and include it with the return. Orders over $100 will be assessed a 20% restocking fee.
  4. Return Address: MVP Brands - Colonial Candle DTC , Attention: Returns Department , 600 Sherwood Drive Union City, TN 38261
  5. For order or shipping issues, please contact the Customer Service Department at (978) 938-0013 or email customerservice@namsteycandle.com with the specific details and we will work with you to correct the issue.
  6. Damaged Product: For product that has arrived damaged, it is not necessary to return the product. Please email a photo of the damaged product, along with your name and order number, and we will be happy to assist you with a replacement order.
