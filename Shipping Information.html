Standard Shipping and Handling.
  1. Most orders ship within 3-5 business days from receipt of order, this excludes weekends. Please note, orders placed during heavily promoted and/or holiday periods might require additional processing time.
  2. Please allow 5-7 business days for shipments within the continental U.S. to arrive. Shipments to Alaska, Hawaii and APO and FPO order can take up to 4 weeks to arrive. 
  3. You can ship an order to a different address than your billing address.  
  4. Physical street addresses are required; no PO boxes can be processed. 
  5. Shipments to Alaska, Hawaii, APO and FPO addresses are Priority Parcel Post delivery only. An additional fee does apply. 
  6. Please note that 'HOT ZONE' shipping is implemented from May 1st through Labor Day in order to avoid melting of candles. Orders shipping to the following states will be shipped on Mondays only unless expedited delivery is requested:
  7.  Washington, Oregon, California, Nevada, Utah, Idaho, Montana, Wyoming, Colorado, Arizona, New Mexico, South Dakota, North Dakota, Nebraska, Texas, and Florida
